What we do in Defense

For mission-critical and ground/shelter systems we apply MIL-standard reliability prediction (MIL-HDBK-217F), FMECA (MIL-STD-1629A), system safety (MIL-STD-882), environmental qualification (MIL-STD-810) and full ILS to the S-series specifications, with rigorous requirements traceability.
