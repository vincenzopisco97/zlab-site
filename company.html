Who We Are

Since 2006, Z Lab has supported manufacturers in the most demanding sectors — rail, aerospace, naval, defense and industry. We bring analytical rigour, deep regulatory fluency and hands-on certification experience to systems where safety and reliability cannot be compromised.

Our engineers work as an extension of your team — from early analysis and modelling through verification, validation and the certification dossier.
